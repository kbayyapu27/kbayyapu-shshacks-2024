"TAKE CARE"

SaLT: Service and Learning Together

The City of Highland Park, Village of Wilmete, and the Village of Winnekta are launching the "Take Care" Packages initiative. These packages aim to support migrants arriving in Chicago suburbs, offering essential items for their well-being and comfort.

Each Package will include:

  • Water
  • Non-perishable Food/Drink
  • Personal Hygiene Products
  • Winter Clothing
  • Baby Items
  • Children's Comfort Items
  • Translation Guides
  • Emergency Kits
  • (Also accept gift card for groceries or/and pharmacies)

Drop-off locations are specified in Highland Park, Wilmete, and Winnetka. The initiative will be ongoing, with a goal of maintaining at least 200 packages available at all times.
