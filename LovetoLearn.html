Welcome to Love2Learn!

Our Services

Love2Learn provides free, monthly, one-hour, one-on-one, math/reading tutoring and learning games for local elementary students.

Caring high school students provide the tutoring services for K-6 children from the Highwood/Highland Park area.

Love2Learn Serves:

  • 100% are residents of Highland Park or Highwood.
  • 100% are low-income.
  • 95% are of a racial minority.
  • All students are grades K-6.
